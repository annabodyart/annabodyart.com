FAQ


  • How long does it take to paint a model?

    Glitter/Airbrush Tattoo and simple Facepaint design usually take around 2/5 of minutes depending on the design. More complex Facepaint design could much more. A full Bodypaint usually don't take more then 6/7 hours. It can eventually take from 3 to 6 hours depdending on the complexity of the design or if I have an assistant.

  • How long does color it last?

    Glitter tattoos: 4/5 days. Airbrush tattoos: around 7/10 days Regular Facepainting/Bodypaint: I use water based colors so they can be removed with water and soap (or simple baby wipes). There are a few color (like some blue or green tones) that have a stronger pigment so to remove them the operation need to repeated more times to get them entirely off. Usually baby oils can help. NOTE: All the information above may vary drastically depending the skin exposure to sun, showers, swimming pool water or other chemicals, etc..

  • Who can get painted?

    Anybody without spefic allergies or skin issues.

  • Is the color safe for the skin?

    I only use the top professional paint brands and I always buy at professional known stores. All these brands are tested for skin safety and are for kids and adults. There are exceptions for anyone with allergies, intollerances and skin problems. Also, I take good and serious care of cleaning and sanitizing all my brushes and colors. For more details see the question below.

  • How do you clean your brushes and sponges?

    Oh, Yes! All my brushes are always cleaned and sanitized with specific products ,also used to sanitize baby care objects, after every session. I also make sure products are correctly dry and correctly stored after the sanitization process.
